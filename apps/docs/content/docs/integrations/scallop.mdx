<Callout type="warn">**Status: planned.** Money-market lending integration on the next-wave list.</Callout>

## What an agent will do on Scallop

- **Supply** a coin into the market (`scallop::market::deposit_collateral`).
- **Borrow** within a borrow cap (custom policy enforcing
  collateralisation ratio).
- **Repay** + withdraw on a rebalance signal.

## Policies that apply

- `spending_limit_policy` — required.
- `borrow_cap_policy` (custom, in design) — caps health factor, max
  notional borrow, etc.
